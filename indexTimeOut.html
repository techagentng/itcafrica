Building Capacity for the African continent

We promote investment in science, technology, engineering and math, and the use of technology in solving problems of matter, energy, healthcare, service delivery, food and physical security as well as learning.

VISION

The vision of ITC-Africa is to serve as the clearinghouse, marketplace and forum for countries and African nationals, people of African heritage and Africa’s partners to discuss, exchange ideas, monitor and evaluate the continent’s efforts and progress in innovation and technology..

MISSION

The mission of ITC-Africa is to promote, secure and sustain Africa’s progress in innovation, technology and socio-economic development.

CORE VALUES

At ITC-Africa, we believe in the and practice the following core values:

  • Apolitical : We do not engage in politics and are not a political organization or institution

  • Communication : We are focused on promoting and elevating Africa’s role and place in innovation and technology by ensuring that all citizens are connected and informed.

  • Integrity : We uphold professionalism and maintain a culture of respect self and for all.

  • Collaboration : We believe in and practice vigorous teamwork.
  • Creativity: We are place a premium on thinking outside the box. Identifying challenges and matching those with solutions are cornerstones of our mandate.
